## What requires a commercial license?

A **commercial license** is required if you or your organisation use Runbound in any
of the following contexts:

- Providing DNS-as-a-service, managed DNS, or any paid network service powered by Runbound
- Integrating Runbound into a commercial product or SaaS offering

- Any use where Runbound contributes — directly or indirectly — to generating revenue

If you are unsure whether your use case requires a commercial license, contact us before
deploying.

## How to obtain a commercial license

Send a request to **redlemonbe@codix.be** with:

1. Your company name and country

3. Estimated number of deployment instances and/or end users

We will respond within **5 business days** with a license proposal and contract.
